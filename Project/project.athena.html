Project Athena

American Express, Gurgaon, Haryana
Duration: Feb'25 - Sept'25

Objective

Migrate legacy .NET payment portal to the cloud-based Hydra platform due to end-of-support, preserving core functionality while upgrading the technology stack.

Key responsibilities

  • End-to-end technology upliftment and migration planning
  • Data validation and reconciliation to ensure integrity post-migration
  • Change management & stakeholder communications
  • End-to-end UI UAT and defect triage
  • PI planning and design documentation

Technologies

Confluence Visio Jira Murale Excel

Achievements

  • Successfully transitioned the application from unsupported legacy technology to a modern, cloud-based platformwith zero business disruption.
  • Improved platform scalability, security, and maintainability, reducing future operational risk.
  • Ensured full stakeholder alignment and user adoption through rigorous UAT and change management processes.
